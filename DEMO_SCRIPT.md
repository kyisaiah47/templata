## Demo Tips

### Key Moments to Highlight:
1. **The number**: 1,300 templates, 104,000 prompts, 26,000 articles (repeat these)
2. **The tagline**: "Wikipedia meets Notion for Life's Biggest Moments" (say it 2-3 times)
3. **The switching**: Show view mode transitions with keyboard shortcuts (feels magical)
4. **The dock**: Open/close the floating dock (shows content is always accessible)
5. **The free model**: Emphasize core features are free forever

### What NOT to Show:
- Dashboard components (you mentioned they're incomplete)
- Any buggy/unfinished features
- Complex settings or edge cases
- Too much time in any one view (keep it moving)

### Emotional Beats:
- **Start**: Empathy (blank page anxiety, everyone's felt it)
- **Middle**: Wonder (look at all this guidance available!)
- **End**: Empowerment (you're ready for your next big moment)

### Call-and-Response Opportunities:
- "How many of you have felt blank page anxiety?" *(everyone raises hand)*
- "What's a big moment you're facing right now?" *(get specific examples)*
- "How do you prefer to organize: chat, board, or list?" *(different thinking styles)*
