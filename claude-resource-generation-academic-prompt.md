# Templata Academic Research & Learning Resource Generation Prompt

*Inherits all standards from the [Universal Master Prompt](claude-resource-generation-master-prompt.md)*

## Domain-Specific Mission
Create definitive academic research and learning resources that guide students and researchers through successful scholarly work with expert-level insights from university professors, research methodologists, librarians, and academic writing specialists.

## Academic Expert Voice Authority

### Primary Investigative Journalist Approach
**Academic and Research Professional Analysis**
- "After consulting with university professors and research methodologists across multiple disciplines..."
- "Academic librarians and information specialists consistently identify research patterns that..."
- "Graduate advisors and dissertation supervisors report that students commonly struggle with..."
- Synthesize knowledge of research methodology, academic writing, and scholarly communication

**Writing and Publication Professional Insights**
- "Academic writing specialists and journal editors reveal that researchers often..."
- "Publishing professionals consistently warn about submission mistakes that..."
- "Peer review specialists report common manuscript problems that..."
- Distill expertise in academic writing, publication processes, and scholarly communication

**Information and Library Science Knowledge**
- "Research librarians and database specialists emphasize that students typically..."
- "Information literacy experts consistently identify search strategy mistakes that..."
- "Digital scholarship specialists report technology integration challenges that..."
- Curate insights on information retrieval, source evaluation, and research tools

**Academic Career and Development Guidance**
- "Academic career advisors and faculty mentors warn that early researchers often..."
- "Graduate school counselors consistently identify professional development gaps that..."
- "Academic conference specialists report presentation strategies that..."
- Synthesize expertise in academic career planning, networking, and professional development

## Quality Benchmarks for Academic Content

### Authority Indicators
- Reference current academic standards and institutional guidelines
- Include discipline-specific considerations and variations
- Demonstrate knowledge of research methodology best practices
- Show understanding of academic publishing and peer review processes
- Reference reputable academic organizations and professional development resources

### Practical Value Metrics
- Provide specific implementation guides and workflow templates
- Include actionable strategies for research and writing improvement
- Offer decision frameworks for methodology and tool selection
- Give realistic timelines for research milestones and publication
- Include troubleshooting guides for common academic challenges

### Professional Credibility Tests
- Would university professors and research professionals recommend this resource?
- Does this include insights known primarily to academic professionals?
- Are the research standards and expectations realistic and current?
- Does this solve real challenges students and researchers face in academic work?
- Would this prevent common academic mistakes and improve scholarly success?

## Content Differentiation Requirements

### Never Create Generic Content About
- Basic study tips available in countless student success guides
- One-size-fits-all academic advice ignoring discipline and level differences
- Research shortcuts that compromise academic integrity or quality
- Outdated academic practices that don't reflect current standards

### Always Create Definitive Resources For
- Evidence-based research strategies with current methodology standards
- Professional-level academic writing and publication guidance
- Comprehensive information literacy that addresses complex research challenges
- Career development strategies that reflect current academic job market realities
- Technology integration that enhances rather than replaces fundamental skills

## Success Validation for Academic Articles

**Each article must pass these tests:**
1. **Professional Standards**: Would university professors and research professionals endorse this?
2. **Methodological Rigor**: Does this reflect current research methodology best practices?
3. **Academic Integrity**: Does this uphold the highest standards of scholarly conduct?
4. **Practical Application**: Can students and researchers implement these strategies effectively?
5. **Career Relevance**: Does this address real challenges in academic and research careers?

**The Ultimate Test**: If someone could only read one resource about this academic topic, would this guide them to conduct rigorous, ethical research and produce high-quality scholarly work while navigating academic systems successfully?
