# Blog Article Generation Guidelines

## Header Requirements

### Length Limits
- **Main headers (##)**: Maximum 4-6 words, ideally 3-4 words
- **Subheaders (###)**: Maximum 3-4 words, ideally 2-3 words
- **Focus on action words and key concepts**

### Examples

❌ **TOO LONG:**
```
## The Complete Process of Researching and Selecting Your New Area for Maximum Success
### Start with the Foundation and Create Your Comprehensive Planning System
```

✅ **CORRECT LENGTH:**
```
## Research Your New Area
### Start with Planning
```

❌ **TOO WORDY:**
```
## Understanding the Complete Timeline Process for Moving Day Preparation
### Essential Tasks and Responsibilities for Week 8
```

✅ **CONCISE AND CLEAR:**
```
## Week 8 Timeline
### Essential Tasks
```

## Content Enhancement Guidelines

### Highlighting Key Information

Use these syntax patterns to make important content stand out:

#### Blue Highlights (`==text==`)
Use for **key concepts, important phrases, and critical information**:
- Main benefits: `==saves 20% on costs==`
- Key numbers: `==8-week timeline==`
- Important concepts: `==proper planning and structured approach==`

#### Purple Underlines (`__text__`)
Use for **specific actionable items, quantities, and precise details**:
- Specific quantities: `__3 moving companies__`, `__20% buffer__`
- Exact numbers: `__6-8 weeks ahead__`
- Specific deadlines: `__by Friday__`

### Content Structure Guidelines

#### Opening Paragraphs
- First paragraph: Hook with problem/pain point
- Second paragraph: Introduce the solution with highlighted key benefit
- Use highlights for the main value proposition

#### Section Content
- **Each section should have 2-4 highlighted/underlined elements**
- Highlight key takeaways and important numbers
- Underline specific actionable items and quantities

#### Examples of Good Usage

```markdown
Moving can be overwhelming, but with ==proper planning== you can make it smooth. This ==8-week timeline== breaks down every task, ensuring you complete everything __on schedule__.

## Week 1: Initial Setup

Start by contacting __at least 3 companies__ for quotes. Set aside ==20% extra budget== for unexpected costs, and begin decluttering __room by room__.

### Budget Planning
Your moving budget should include ==all major expenses== plus a __20% contingency fund__. Most families spend between ==$2,000-$5,000== depending on distance and services.
```

## Quality Standards

### Header Readability
- Headers must fit comfortably in the table of contents sidebar
- No line breaks or overflow in TOC navigation
- Users should understand the section at a glance

### Content Highlighting
- **Minimum 8-12 highlights per article**
- **Balance between == highlights and __ underlines**
- Don't over-highlight - maintain readability
- Highlight the most important 20% of content

### Testing Guidelines
- Check TOC sidebar for header length after generation
- Ensure headers are scannable and meaningful
- Verify highlights enhance rather than distract from reading

## Template for Article Structure

```markdown
# Article Title (Keep Under 8 Words)

Opening paragraph with ==key benefit highlighted==...

## Short Header (3-4 words max)

Content with ==important concepts== and __specific details__ highlighted.

### Brief Subheader (2-3 words)

More content with strategic highlighting of ==key points== and __actionable items__.

## Next Short Header

Continue pattern with balanced highlighting...
```

## Validation Checklist

Before publishing any blog article:

- [ ] All headers are under the word limits
- [ ] Headers fit properly in TOC sidebar
- [ ] 8-12 strategic highlights/underlines used
- [ ] Key numbers and concepts are highlighted
- [ ] Specific actions and quantities are underlined
- [ ] Content flows naturally despite highlighting
- [ ] TOC navigation is clean and scannable
